Buffalo Sauce

This recipe is modified from:
http://www.wikihow.com/Make-Buffalo-Sauce

Ingredients:

-1/4 cup Frank's Hot Sauce
-1/4 cup sriracha
-1/2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
-1/4 tsp garlic powder
-1/2 tsp celery salt
-4 tbsp butter

Directions:

Mix together wet ingredients with garlic powder and celery salt

Melt butter on stove over medium heat

Whisk mixed ingredients into butter and bring to low boil

Lower heat and simmer for 10 minutes
