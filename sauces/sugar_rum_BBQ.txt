Sugar Rum BBQ Sauce

This recipe is modified from:
http://allrecipes.com/Recipe/Barbecue-Ribs/Detail.aspx

Ingredients:

-1 cup brown sugar
-1/4 cup ketchup
-1/4 cup soy sauce
-1/4 cup Worcestershire sauce
-1/4 cup rum
-1/2 cup chile sauce
-2 cloves garlic, crushed
-1 teaspoon course mustard
-1 dash ground black pepper

Directions:

Mix it all up.
