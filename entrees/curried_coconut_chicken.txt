Curried Coconut Chicken

Modified from: http://allrecipes.com/recipe/curried-coconut-chicken/detail.aspx

Ingredients:

-2 pounds boneless skinless chicken breasts, cut into 1/2-inch chunks 
-1 teaspoon salt and pepper, or to taste 
-1 1/2 tablespoons vegetable oil (olive oil is OK)
-2 tablespoons curry powder 
-1/2 onion, thinly sliced 
-2 cloves garlic, crushed
-1 (14 ounce) can coconut milk
-1 (14.5 ounce) can stewed, diced tomatoes
-3 tablespoons sugar 


Method:

Heat oil on medium-high heat. Add curry and cook for 5 minutes.

Reduce heat to medium. Add salt, pepper, and onions and cook until translucent.

Add garlic and cook for a minute.

Add stewed tomatoes with juice. Cook for another 5 minutes.

Add chicken. Cook for 7-10 minutes, until chicken is cooked through.

Mix sugar into coconut milk and add to pot. Stir until mixed. Cook 30-40 minutes uncovered.
